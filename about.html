About Condor Virtual

Condor Virtual is a community-driven virtual airline inspired by the real Condor Flugdienst GmbH. We replicate real-world operations, offering pilots realistic flight planning, dispatch, and tracking across global destinations.

Our fleet reflects the real Condor lineup, including the Airbus A330-900neo, A321, and Boeing 757-300. With over 90 destinations worldwide, we provide immersive and structured routes for both scheduled and charter services.

Whether you're new or experienced, Condor Virtual offers community events, active staff, and professional standards. Join us to share the passion for virtual aviation in a structured and friendly environment.
